Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a procurement request?

To submit a procurement request, navigate to the Procurement Portal and click "New Request". Fill out the required information including items needed, justification, and approver. Once submitted, you'll receive a confirmation email with your request ID.

How can I track my order status?

You can track your order status in the Procurement Portal under "My Requests" or by using the order tracking number provided in your confirmation email. Real-time updates are available throughout the procurement process.

What is the approval process for requests?

Requests go through a multi-stage approval process: 1) Department manager approval, 2) Budget verification, 3) Procurement team review, 4) Final approval and ordering. The process typically takes 2-5 business days depending on the request value and complexity.

How do I add new assets to inventory?

New assets can be added through the Inventory Management section. Click "Add Asset" and provide details including asset name, category, location, and assigned user. The system will automatically generate an asset ID and QR code for tracking.

What are the spending limits for different departments?

Spending limits vary by department and are set annually. Individual requests over $5,000 require additional approval. Department managers can view their current budget allocation and remaining balance in the Analytics Dashboard.

How do I report a technical issue?

Technical issues can be reported through the support chat, email, or phone. Please include your employee ID, a description of the issue, and any error messages. For urgent issues, use the phone support option for immediate assistance.
